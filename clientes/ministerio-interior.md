# Ministerio del Interior — VC-WDP

The Ministerio del Interior (Ministry of the Interior) of Spain is a central government ministry managing national security forces, border control, civil protection, and penitentiary infrastructure. This page documents the **Wire Data Engineer (VC-WDP)** training engagement. The ministry also received VC-LDA (Data Engineer) training, documented in [Mininterior Lda](mininterior-lda.md).

## Course Delivered

The training delivered was [Vc Wde](../formacion/vc-wde.md) (formerly VC-WDP — Wire Data Engineer), the 8-hour course covering [Viewtimon Overview](../productos/viewtimon-overview.md), DPI traffic analysis, and wire data management. This is the most complete VC-WDP delivery in the corpus, with full dual-format materials (PDFs + PPTs) following the [Vc Wde Agenda](../cursos/vc-wde-agenda.md) completely.

## Training Materials

The project folder contains **7 PDFs** and **7 PPTs** (14 files total), making it the most comprehensive Wire Data training package:

This 7-module set exceeds the standard [Vc Wde Agenda](../cursos/vc-wde-agenda.md), adding [Viewtilog Overview](../productos/viewtilog-overview.md) and [Viewtisight Overview](../productos/viewtisight-overview.md) modules not present in the [Gbo Balear](gbo-balear.md) 5-module package.

## Notable Aspects

- **Dual-format materials (PDF + PPT)**: The availability of both formats mirrors the [Giss](giss.md) and [Junta Andalucia](junta-andalucia.md) approach in the VC-LDA track, suggesting a formal, structured training with pre-reading and instructor materials.
- **Extended 7-module set**: Including [Viewtilog Overview](../productos/viewtilog-overview.md) (module 06) and [Viewtisight Overview](../productos/viewtisight-overview.md) (module 07) in a Wire Data course is unusual and indicates cross-track training — exposing attendees to [Etl Pipeline](../conceptos/etl-pipeline.md) and analytics capabilities beyond standard VC-WDP scope.
- **Deployment Mode module** covers inline vs. sniffer deployment for [Viewtimon Overview](../productos/viewtimon-overview.md) and [Viewtiqos Overview](../productos/viewtiqos-overview.md), including [Standalone Vs Cluster](../conceptos/standalone-vs-cluster.md) considerations.
- [Viewtimanager Overview](../productos/viewtimanager-overview.md) content covers [Inventory Management](../configuracion/inventory-management.md), [Autodiscovery](../configuracion/autodiscovery.md), [Users](../configuracion/users.md), [Roles](../configuracion/roles.md), and [Tenants](../configuracion/tenants.md) for enterprise-scale security infrastructure.
- The ministry's dual-track engagement (VC-LDA + VC-WDP) is unique in the entire training corpus.
