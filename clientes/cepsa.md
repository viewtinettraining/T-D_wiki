# CEPSA

CEPSA (Compania Espanola de Petroleos) is a major Spanish multinational energy company operating in oil and gas exploration, refining, chemicals, and service stations. As a critical infrastructure company, CEPSA requires robust network observability and data engineering capabilities across its distributed operations.

## Course Delivered

The training delivered was [[vc-de]] (formerly VC-LDA — Certified Data Engineer), the 16-hour technical course covering the full Viewtinet data engineering stack. Students are expected to hold [[vc-oa]] certification before attending. The course focuses on [[viewtimanager-overview]], [[viewtilog-overview]], and [[viewtisight-overview]].

## Notable Aspects

- Energy sector deployments typically involve heterogeneous network environments with both OT and IT assets, making [[autodiscovery]] and [[inventory-management]] critical capabilities.
- Viewtilog training covers [[etl-pipeline]] construction and the [[vs-data-broker-overview]] for ingesting diverse data sources.
- Viewtimanager configuration includes [[users]], [[roles]], and [[tenants]] management for multi-site environments.
- The absence of a dedicated Welcome/Products module suggests the client may have had prior Viewtinet exposure.
