# Cursos/Projects Corpus — Customer Training Files

This page summarizes all customer training project files found in the `Cursos/Projects/` directory. Each subdirectory represents a distinct training engagement delivered by Viewtinet to an enterprise client. Files consist of PowerPoint presentations (PPTX), PDF exports, workbook PDFs, and supplementary CSV files (attendance or inventory records).

The corpus spans two course tracks: [Vc De](../formacion/vc-de.md) (Data Engineer, formerly VC-LDA, 16h) and [Vc Wde](../formacion/vc-wde.md) (Wire Data Engineer, formerly VC-WDP, 8h). Both tracks build on [Vc Oa](../formacion/vc-oa.md) foundational certification.
