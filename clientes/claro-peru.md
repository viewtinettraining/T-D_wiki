# CLARO Peru

CLARO Peru is the Peruvian subsidiary of America Movil, one of Latin America's largest telecommunications groups. As a major telecom operator providing mobile, fixed broadband, and pay-TV services in Peru, CLARO Peru manages high-volume network traffic where [[viewtilog-overview]] and [[viewtimon-overview]] capabilities are especially relevant.

## Course Delivered

The training delivered was [[vc-de]] (formerly VC-LDA — Certified Data Engineer), covering the Viewtinet data engineering stack. This is a technically demanding course requiring [[vc-oa]] prerequisite certification. Telecom operators are a primary target audience for Viewtinet's [[etl-pipeline]] and [[vs-data-broker-overview]] capabilities due to their need to process massive volumes of network telemetry.
