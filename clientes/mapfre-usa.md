# MAPFRE USA

MAPFRE USA is the North American subsidiary of MAPFRE, one of Spain's largest insurance groups and a major global insurer. Operating in the United States insurance market, MAPFRE USA manages IT infrastructure supporting claims processing, policy management, and regulatory compliance — environments where network observability and data engineering are critical.

## Course Delivered

The training delivered was [Vc De](../formacion/vc-de.md) (formerly VC-LDA — Certified Data Engineer), the full 16-hour course. Students must hold [Vc Oa](../formacion/vc-oa.md) certification as a prerequisite. With 9 modules, MAPFRE USA received one of the most complete VC-LDA PPT-only deliveries in the corpus, second only to [Giss](giss.md) which also includes PDFs and a workbook.

## Notable Aspects

- **Viewtiauth module** (module 05) covers [Ldap Integration](../integraciones/ldap-integration.md), [Active Directory](../integraciones/active-directory.md), [Mfa Configuration](../integraciones/mfa-configuration.md), and authentication backend configuration — essential for enterprise insurance environments with strict identity governance.
- **Deployment Mode module** covers [Standalone Vs Cluster](../conceptos/standalone-vs-cluster.md) decisions and [Ha Architecture](../conceptos/ha-architecture.md) for high-availability deployments, critical for financial sector SLA requirements.
- **LAB Access module** provides hands-on access to the Viewtinet environment, supporting the practical component of the [Vc De Agenda](../cursos/vc-de-agenda.md).
- [Viewtimanager Overview](../productos/viewtimanager-overview.md) covers [Users](../configuracion/users.md), [Roles](../configuracion/roles.md), [Tenants](../configuracion/tenants.md), and [Autodiscovery](../configuracion/autodiscovery.md) for enterprise multi-site management.
- [Viewtilog Overview](../productos/viewtilog-overview.md) and [Vs Data Broker Overview](../productos/vs-data-broker-overview.md) address the [Etl Pipeline](../conceptos/etl-pipeline.md) capabilities for processing insurance data flows and network telemetry.
- The US-based location of this client reflects Viewtinet's international commercial reach.
