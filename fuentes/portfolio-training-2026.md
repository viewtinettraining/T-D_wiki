# Viewtinet Training Portfolio 2026 v1.4

This page summarizes the Viewtinet Training Portfolio 2026 document (version 1.4), which defines the official certification program structure, course catalog, learning objectives, and assessment criteria for all Viewtinet training offerings.

## Document Reference

- **Title:** Viewtinet Training Portfolio 2026
- **Version:** 1.4
- **Year:** 2026
- **Type:** Official internal training catalog

## Purpose

The Training Portfolio 2026 v1.4 is the authoritative reference for all Viewtinet certification programs. It defines five certification verticals, course durations, delivery formats, prerequisites, learning objectives, and examination requirements. For the program overview and mission, see [Training Overview](../formacion/training-overview.md).

## Courses Documented

### Sales Vertical
- **[Vsp](../formacion/vsp.md)** — Viewtinet Sales Professional (1 hour, online, no exam)

### Presales Vertical
- **[Vc Psp](../formacion/vc-psp.md)** — Viewtinet Certified Presales Solutions Professional (8 hours, online/on-site, 70% exam required)

### ViewtiSight Vertical
- **[Vc Oa](../formacion/vc-oa.md)** — Viewtinet Certified Observability Analyst / VCOA (16 hours, online/on-site, 70% exam required). **Mandatory analytical core.** See [Vcoa Modules](../cursos/vcoa-modules.md) for the detailed 6-module breakdown.

### Viewtilog Vertical
- **[Vc De](../formacion/vc-de.md)** — Viewtinet Certified Data Engineer (16 hours, online/on-site, 70% exam required, requires [Vc Oa](../formacion/vc-oa.md)). Formerly named VC-LDA. See [Vc De Agenda](../cursos/vc-de-agenda.md) for the full agenda.

### ViewtiMon & Viewtify Vertical
- **[Vc Wde](../formacion/vc-wde.md)** — Viewtinet Wire Data Engineer / VC-WDP (8 hours, online/on-site, 70% exam required). See [Vc Wde Agenda](../cursos/vc-wde-agenda.md) for the full agenda.

## Certification Path Structure

The portfolio establishes a clear progression model. See [Certification Paths](../formacion/certification-paths.md) for the full diagram. Key rules:

1. [Vsp](../formacion/vsp.md) and [Vc Psp](../formacion/vc-psp.md) are standalone commercial tracks
2. [Vc Oa](../formacion/vc-oa.md) is the mandatory prerequisite for all advanced technical certifications
3. After [Vc Oa](../formacion/vc-oa.md), professionals may specialize in [Vc De](../formacion/vc-de.md) (Viewtilog) or [Vc Wde](../formacion/vc-wde.md) (ViewtiMon & ViewtifyQoS)

## Platform Products Referenced

The portfolio covers the full Viewtinet suite: [Viewtisight Overview](../productos/viewtisight-overview.md), [Viewtilog Overview](../productos/viewtilog-overview.md), [Viewtimon Overview](../productos/viewtimon-overview.md), [Viewtiqos Overview](../productos/viewtiqos-overview.md), and [Viewtimanager Overview](../productos/viewtimanager-overview.md). The [Vs Data Broker Overview](../productos/vs-data-broker-overview.md) is covered in depth within the [Vc De](../formacion/vc-de.md) track.

## Assessment Standards

All technical certifications require a minimum score of **70%** on an Examination Presentation. The [Vsp](../formacion/vsp.md) is the only course without a formal assessment.

## Versions and History

- v1.4 (2026): Current version. Introduced the VCOA "Analytical Core" framing and formally renamed VC-LDA to [Vc De](../formacion/vc-de.md). Also standardized the VC-WDE/VC-WDP naming.
- Previous versions referenced VC-LDA and VC-WDE as separate designations.
