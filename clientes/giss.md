# GISS

GISS is a technology and IT services organization that received the most complete VC-LDA training package in the Viewtinet corpus. The full dual-format material set (PDFs + PPTs) with an accompanying workbook indicates a formal, rigorous training engagement intended for technical teams requiring hands-on certification preparation.

## Course Delivered

The training delivered was [[vc-de]] (formerly VC-LDA — Certified Data Engineer), the full 16-hour course. This is a prerequisite-gated course requiring [[vc-oa]] certification. The GISS engagement covers all standard modules plus [[viewtiauth|Viewtiauth authentication integration]], which is not present in all client packages. Students also received a structured lab environment via the LAB Access module.

## Training Materials

GISS has the most complete material set in the VC-LDA track: **7 PDFs + 7 PPTs + 1 Workbook PDF** (16 files total):

The workbook (`viewtinet-workbooks-new_en_v6.3.5`) provides guided exercises aligned with the [[vc-de-agenda]] practical labs.

## Notable Aspects

- The inclusion of a **Viewtiauth module** covers [[ldap-integration]], [[active-directory]], [[mfa-configuration]], and authentication backend configuration — topics absent from shorter client packages.
- The **Software Architecture module** covers [[standalone-vs-cluster]] deployment modes, [[ha-architecture]], and [[installation-bundle]] concepts.
- The **LAB Access module** provides structured access to a practical environment, essential for the hands-on [[etl-pipeline]] and [[viewtilog-installation]] exercises.
- The workbook format mirrors the [[mapfre-usa]] level of completeness in the VC-LDA track.
- [[viewtimanager-overview]] content covers [[users]], [[roles]], [[tenants]], and [[autodiscovery]].

## Related Clients

[[mapfre-usa]] is the other VC-LDA client with a similarly full module set (9 PPTs including Viewtiauth). [[junta-andalucia]] also has an extensive PDF-based delivery. GISS stands out as the only client with both full dual-format materials and a workbook PDF.
