# CLARO Peru

CLARO Peru is the Peruvian subsidiary of America Movil, one of Latin America's largest telecommunications groups. As a major telecom operator providing mobile, fixed broadband, and pay-TV services in Peru, CLARO Peru manages high-volume network traffic where [Viewtilog Overview](../productos/viewtilog-overview.md) and [Viewtimon Overview](../productos/viewtimon-overview.md) capabilities are especially relevant.

## Course Delivered

The training delivered was [Vc De](../formacion/vc-de.md) (formerly VC-LDA — Certified Data Engineer), covering the Viewtinet data engineering stack. This is a technically demanding course requiring [Vc Oa](../formacion/vc-oa.md) prerequisite certification. Telecom operators are a primary target audience for Viewtinet's [Etl Pipeline](../conceptos/etl-pipeline.md) and [Vs Data Broker Overview](../productos/vs-data-broker-overview.md) capabilities due to their need to process massive volumes of network telemetry.
