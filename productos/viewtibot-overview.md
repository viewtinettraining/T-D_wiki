# ViewtiBot Overview

**Viewtibot** is a custom-trained GPT assistant designed exclusively for the Viewtinet product ecosystem. It provides precise, step-by-step guidance for configuration tasks, troubleshooting procedures, and operational questions across all Viewtinet modules.

## Access

Viewtibot is accessible online at:

**https://chatgpt.com/g/g-680f084439c48191b9b29ed7a39998e9-viewtibot**

A ChatGPT account is required to interact with the assistant.

## Key Features

- **Product-specific knowledge**: Trained exclusively on Viewtinet documentation, ensuring answers align with the platform's modules, terminology, and best practices — covering [Viewtimanager](viewtimanager-overview.md), [Viewtilog](viewtilog-overview.md), [ViewtiSight](viewtisight-overview.md), [ViewtiMon](viewtimon-overview.md), and others.
- **Step-by-step guidance**: Delivers clear, actionable configuration and troubleshooting instructions.
- **Consistency**: Responses follow official Viewtinet recommendations.
- **Always available**: Online 24/7, making it useful for both training and production environments.

## Use Cases

- **Configuration Assistance**: Quickly obtain configuration steps for modules such as [VS Data Broker](vs-data-broker-overview.md), [Viewtimanager](viewtimanager-overview.md), and [ViewtiSight](viewtisight-overview.md).
- **Troubleshooting**: Diagnose and resolve issues across the platform with guided procedures.
- **Training and Certification**: Acts as a study companion for professionals undergoing Viewtinet training.
- **Operational Efficiency**: Reduces time spent searching manuals by providing instant contextual answers.

## How to Use

1. Navigate to the Viewtibot URL above.
2. Log in with a ChatGPT account.
3. Enter a question or describe the procedure needed.
4. Viewtibot returns tailored step-by-step guidance or references specific configuration processes.

## Important Notice

Viewtibot is a **supplementary tool** only. It does not replace official Viewtinet documentation or the Viewtinet [support](../configuracion/roles.md) team. For complex issues, product updates, or advanced troubleshooting, always refer to the official manuals or contact Viewtinet support directly.
