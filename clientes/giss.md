# GISS

GISS is a technology and IT services organization that received the most complete VC-LDA training package in the Viewtinet corpus. The full dual-format material set (PDFs + PPTs) with an accompanying workbook indicates a formal, rigorous training engagement intended for technical teams requiring hands-on certification preparation.

## Course Delivered

The training delivered was [Vc De](../formacion/vc-de.md) (formerly VC-LDA — Certified Data Engineer), the full 16-hour course. This is a prerequisite-gated course requiring [Vc Oa](../formacion/vc-oa.md) certification. The GISS engagement covers all standard modules plus [Viewtiauth authentication integration](../integraciones/viewtiauth.md), which is not present in all client packages. Students also received a structured lab environment via the LAB Access module.

## Training Materials

GISS has the most complete material set in the VC-LDA track: **7 PDFs + 7 PPTs + 1 Workbook PDF** (16 files total):

The workbook (`viewtinet-workbooks-new_en_v6.3.5`) provides guided exercises aligned with the [Vc De Agenda](../cursos/vc-de-agenda.md) practical labs.

## Notable Aspects

- The inclusion of a **Viewtiauth module** covers [Ldap Integration](../integraciones/ldap-integration.md), [Active Directory](../integraciones/active-directory.md), [Mfa Configuration](../integraciones/mfa-configuration.md), and authentication backend configuration — topics absent from shorter client packages.
- The **Software Architecture module** covers [Standalone Vs Cluster](../conceptos/standalone-vs-cluster.md) deployment modes, [Ha Architecture](../conceptos/ha-architecture.md), and [Installation Bundle](../instalacion/installation-bundle.md) concepts.
- The **LAB Access module** provides structured access to a practical environment, essential for the hands-on [Etl Pipeline](../conceptos/etl-pipeline.md) and [Viewtilog Installation](../instalacion/viewtilog-installation.md) exercises.
- The workbook format mirrors the [Mapfre Usa](mapfre-usa.md) level of completeness in the VC-LDA track.
- [Viewtimanager Overview](../productos/viewtimanager-overview.md) content covers [Users](../configuracion/users.md), [Roles](../configuracion/roles.md), [Tenants](../configuracion/tenants.md), and [Autodiscovery](../configuracion/autodiscovery.md).

## Related Clients

[Mapfre Usa](mapfre-usa.md) is the other VC-LDA client with a similarly full module set (9 PPTs including Viewtiauth). [Junta Andalucia](junta-andalucia.md) also has an extensive PDF-based delivery. GISS stands out as the only client with both full dual-format materials and a workbook PDF.
