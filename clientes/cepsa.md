# CEPSA

CEPSA (Compania Espanola de Petroleos) is a major Spanish multinational energy company operating in oil and gas exploration, refining, chemicals, and service stations. As a critical infrastructure company, CEPSA requires robust network observability and data engineering capabilities across its distributed operations.

## Course Delivered

The training delivered was [Vc De](../formacion/vc-de.md) (formerly VC-LDA — Certified Data Engineer), the 16-hour technical course covering the full Viewtinet data engineering stack. Students are expected to hold [Vc Oa](../formacion/vc-oa.md) certification before attending. The course focuses on [Viewtimanager Overview](../productos/viewtimanager-overview.md), [Viewtilog Overview](../productos/viewtilog-overview.md), and [Viewtisight Overview](../productos/viewtisight-overview.md).

## Notable Aspects

- Energy sector deployments typically involve heterogeneous network environments with both OT and IT assets, making [Autodiscovery](../configuracion/autodiscovery.md) and [Inventory Management](../configuracion/inventory-management.md) critical capabilities.
- Viewtilog training covers [Etl Pipeline](../conceptos/etl-pipeline.md) construction and the [Vs Data Broker Overview](../productos/vs-data-broker-overview.md) for ingesting diverse data sources.
- Viewtimanager configuration includes [Users](../configuracion/users.md), [Roles](../configuracion/roles.md), and [Tenants](../configuracion/tenants.md) management for multi-site environments.
- The absence of a dedicated Welcome/Products module suggests the client may have had prior Viewtinet exposure.
